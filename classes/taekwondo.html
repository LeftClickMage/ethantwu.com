What is LCMTKD?

LCMTKD is a form of Taekwondo that Ethan created, which comes from "LeftClickMage" (his YouTube channel) and "TKD"(Taekwondo).

Point-TKD (the style taught at most dojos) is focused on the sport, imagine soccer or football, instead of the self-defense aspect.

LCMTKD is a combination of many different art styles, with the majority being TKD and Muay Thai, crafted with DEFENSE and FUN in mind!

Why learn LCMTKD?

Learning LCMTKD promotes mobility and strength gain, as well as increased self-confidence and respect.

LCMTKD is a great form of exercise, as you can practice what you learn by yourself during lunch, break, or work!

LCMTKD also increases your self-defense ability and overall mood!
